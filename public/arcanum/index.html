Arcanum: Of Steamworks and Magick Obscura

Arcanum: Of Steamworks and Magick Obscura is a role-playing video game developed by Troika Games and published by Sierra On-Line in 2001. It is set in a fictional world where technology and magic coexist. The player character can choose to be a human, an elf, a half-orc, or a dwarf, and can specialize in a variety of skills and abilities.

Arcanum was praised for its innovative gameplay, which combined elements of traditional role-playing games with steampunk and fantasy themes. It also featured a complex and well-written story, and a diverse cast of characters.

If you're a fan of role-playing games, or if you're just looking for a unique and engaging experience, then I highly recommend checking out Arcanum: Of Steamworks and Magick Obscura.
